Projects

Research projects and open-source work in AI security, deepfake detection, and synthetic media forensics.

Real-Time Puppeteering Defense

First enrollment-free defense against identity puppeteering in AI-based videoconferencing. 97.7% AUC at 75 FPS. Published at NeurIPS 2025 in collaboration with NVIDIA Research.

AI-Generated Video Detection

End-to-end pipeline for detecting AI-generated videos with 99%+ accuracy across Sora, Runway AI, Stable Video Diffusion, and NeRF-based systems. Published at CVPR 2024.

Deepfake Detection API

Production-ready deepfake detection system built with Vision Transformer (ViT) and FastAPI. Deployable REST API for real-time face authenticity verification.

Low-Bandwidth Puppeteering Defense

First defense against puppeteering attacks in low-bandwidth talking head videoconferencing systems. 98.03% detection accuracy. Published at CVPR 2023.

GAN Face Detection via Semantic Inconsistencies

GAN-generated face detector leveraging facial semantic inconsistencies across eyes, mouth, and hair regions. 91.36% accuracy with robustness to JPEG compression. Published at Electronic Imaging 2023.
